## Recommended Verification Workflow

Before relying on any regulatory information retrieved through this server:

1. **Identify the relevant IS standard or rule** using this server's search tools
2. **Retrieve the official document** — purchase from BIS at https://www.services.bis.gov.in/ for standards; download from MeitY/CERT-In/RBI/SEBI for public rules
3. **Confirm the version and effective date** — check whether the standard has been revised or superseded
4. **Read the full original text** — do not rely solely on summaries or metadata
5. **Consult qualified legal or compliance counsel** for interpretation and application to your specific facts
